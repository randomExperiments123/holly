# Design Decisions — Prototype 050

## Concept
The grand finale prototype — a full-blown celebration. Confetti rains across the screen, gold sparkles shimmer, and bold typography announces this as a party. The 50th prototype deserves to go all out.

## Layout
- Full-screen dark hero with animated confetti overlay
- Single-column project list with festive accents
- Streamer decorations between sections
- Bold, oversized typography

## Typography
Orbitron in bold weights conveys a celebratory, display-oriented feel. Its futuristic, rounded geometric forms work well with the party theme.

## Animation
- CSS keyframe animated confetti falling from the top
- Sparkling gold particles via pseudo-elements
- Pulsing glow effects on headings
- Bouncing decorative elements

## Color Palette
Five vibrant celebration colors (red, yellow, green, blue, purple) create maximum energy and festive impact against a dark background.

## Lightbox
Dark overlay with confetti-pattern border, gold-accented controls, and celebratory counter display.

## Accessibility Note
All animations respect prefers-reduced-motion by disabling movement.
