# Design Decisions — Prototype 098

## Concept
A Jazz Age speakeasy portfolio dripping with Art Deco glamour. The design transports viewers to the Roaring 20s — geometric gold patterns, moody dark lighting, champagne sparkle, and jazz silhouettes.

## Layout
- Dark moody hero with art deco geometric border frame
- Two-column diamond-grid project layout
- Gold chevron and fan decorations between sections
- Champagne sparkle particles floating overhead
- Saxophone silhouette as decorative element

## Typography
"Playfair Display" in bold italic for elegant, dramatic headings. "Cormorant Garamond" for classic literary body text. Geometric sans for small accents.

## Color Palette
Rich black (#0a0a0a) and deep teal (#1a3a3a) create the speakeasy darkness. Champagne (#f7e7ce) and gold (#c9a84c) provide the glamorous contrast. Burgundy (#722f37) adds depth.

## Decorative Elements
- Art deco geometric borders (chevrons, stepped patterns) via CSS gradients
- Gold foil line accents on section dividers
- Sparkle animations (tiny gold dots via box-shadow)
- Fan/sunburst corner decorations
- Diamond-shaped project card accents

## Lightbox
Moody dark backdrop with gold deco border, champagne-colored text, geometric frame pattern.

## Accessibility Note
Sufficient contrast on text despite dark theme. All animations respect prefers-reduced-motion.
