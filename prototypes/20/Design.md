# Design Document — Prototype 020

## Concept
Pure monochrome minimalism. No colors, no gradients, no ornament — just black, white, gray, and typography. Inspired by Swiss design, Dieter Rams, and the power of restraint. Every element must earn its place through typographic hierarchy and spatial relationships alone.

## Color Palette
- Black: #000000
- White: #ffffff
- Light gray: #cccccc
- Medium gray: #999999
- Dark gray: #333333
- Absolutely no other colors anywhere

## Typography
- Display/Headings: 'Playfair Display', serif (for elegance and contrast)
- Body/Nav: 'Inter', sans-serif (clean, functional)
- Large typographic scale for headings
- Capitalized navigation with generous letter-spacing
- All text in black or gray

## Layout
- Full-width white background
- Hero with ultra-large Playfair Display name — typography as hero
- Single-column project layout (editorial feel)
- Thin 1px black borders and horizontal rules as dividers
- Minimal card: just a border and text — images speak
- Footer with fine print

## Key Interactions
- Hover: subtle opacity change on cards
- Lightbox: grayscale-themed
- Clean, no-frills interactions
