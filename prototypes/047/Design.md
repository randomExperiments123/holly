# Design Decisions — Prototype 047

## Concept
Channeling the optimistic spirit of 1950s Mid-Century Modern design — atomic age motifs, warm retro colors, and playful geometric patterns create a fun, vintage feel.

## Layout
- Centered single column with decorative borders
- Alternating card layouts (image left/right on desktop)
- Starburst decorations in hero
- Patterned section dividers

## Typography
Playfair Display brings sophisticated mid-century editorial style to headings. Inter keeps body text clean and readable.

## Color Palette
The signature mid-century palette of mustard yellow, burnt orange, avocado green, and dark teal creates warm, nostalgic feel.

## Decorative Elements
- CSS-only starburst shapes
- Atomic dot patterns
- Boomerang shapes via clip-path
- Geometric diamond borders
- Retro-wavy underlines

## Lightbox
Warm-toned overlay with retro-styled frame and period-appropriate typography for controls.
