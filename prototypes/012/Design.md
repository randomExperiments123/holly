# Design Document — Prototype 012

## Concept
Brutalist raw concrete. Rejects polish in favour of honesty and structure. Heavy borders, monochrome palette, and utilitarian grid reflect the brutalist ethos of truth to materials.

## Color Palette
- Background: #b2b09b (concrete gray)
- Surface: #c4c2ad (lighter concrete)
- Text: #1a1a1a (near black)
- Accent: #000 (pure black)
- Secondary: #8b8978 (darker concrete)

## Typography
- 'IBM Plex Mono' from Google Fonts exclusively
- Monospace throughout for industrial feel
- Hero: clamp(48px, 10vw, 96px), heavy weight
- No letter-spacing refinement

## Layout
- Full-bleed sections
- Everything has thick black borders
- Project cards: square aspect, heavy frame
- No background on images (raw display)
- Underlined links with double borders
- Asymmetric grid intentionally rough

## Key Interactions
- Heavy black outline hover (no subtle transitions)
- Instant lightbox (no fade)
- Strikethrough effects on links
- Standard lightbox for project images
