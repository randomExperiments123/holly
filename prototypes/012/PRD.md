# Product Requirements Document — Prototype 012

## Overview
Brutalist / Raw Concrete portfolio for Holly Matthews. Unpolished, utilitarian design inspired by brutalist architecture. Heavy black borders, raw typography, intentionally ugly aesthetic.

## Design Direction
- Inspiration: Brutalist architecture (Barbican, Boston City Hall), raw concrete textures
- No rounded corners anywhere
- Heavy black borders (4-8px)
- Utilitarian, anti-design aesthetic
- Intentionally unpolished and raw

## Content
- 5 design projects: Nordica Coffee, Urban Perspectives, Motion & Interaction, Botanic, Dreamscapes
- About section
- Contact section
- Download portfolio button

## Technical Requirements
- Pure HTML + CSS, zero JS dependencies
- Inline lightbox with minimal inline JS
- AVIF images
- Responsive mobile-first
- Accessible
- SEO-optimized with Schema.org
