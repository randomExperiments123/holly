# Product Requirements Document — Prototype 015

## Overview
Memphis Milano-themed portfolio for Holly Matthews. 1980s Memphis design aesthetic with bold colors, squiggly lines, geometric patterns, and playful energy. Inspired by Ettore Sottsass and the Memphis Group.

- Inspiration: Memphis Group (Ettore Sottsass), 80s postmodern design
- Bright, clashing colors
- Wavy borders, dots, triangles, squiggly lines
- Playful, chaotic, maximalist
- Bold geometric shapes as decoration

## Content

- About section
- Contact section
- Download portfolio button

- Accessible
