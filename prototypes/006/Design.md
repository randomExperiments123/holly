# Design Document — Prototype 006

## Concept
Swiss International Style — form follows function. Strict grids, objective photography presentation, clear hierarchy.

## Color Palette
- Background: #ffffff
- Text: #000000 (primary), #666 (secondary)
- Accent: #ff0000 (Swiss red)
- Additional: #f5f5f5 (light gray surfaces)

## Typography
- 'Inter' (tight, grotesk feel)
- All-caps for labels and categories
- Size: 72px hero, 11px uppercase labels
- Tight tracking for uppercase

## Layout
- 12-column grid
- Structured asymmetry
- Bauhaus-inspired geometric elements
- Diagonal lines and geometric shapes
- Photo-forward presentation
