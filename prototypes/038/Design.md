# Design Document — Prototype 038

## Concept
Cottagecore brings the warmth and comfort of countryside living to the portfolio. It feels like browsing a scrapbook in a cozy cottage by the fire.

## Color Palette
- Cream: #f5ebe0
- Warm brown: #8d6e63
- Forest green: #2e5c3a
- Tan: #d4a373
- Deep cream: #faf3e8
- Text: #3e2723 (dark brown)

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif, romantic)
- Body: Inter (clean, readable)
- Handcrafted feel

## Layout
- Soft, organic layout with varied card widths
- Gingham checkered banner backgrounds
- Decorative botanical-style dividers
- Warm, nested framing

## Key Visual Elements
- Gingham/checkered pattern via CSS repeating-linear-gradient
- Botanical divider motifs (CSS leaf shapes)
- Warm shadows and gentle borders
- Rounded, soft corners
- Natural texture overlays
