# Product Requirements Document — Prototype 001

## Overview
Apple-inspired minimalist portfolio for Holly Matthews. Ultra-clean, content-first design emphasizing whitespace, typography, and photographic imagery.

## Design Direction
- Inspiration: apple.com product pages
- Minimalist with maximal impact
- Dark header/nav transitioning to light content
- Large typography as visual element
- Subtle parallax and reveal animations

## Content
- 5 design projects: Nordica Coffee, Urban Perspectives, Motion & Interaction, Botanic, Dreamscapes
- About section
- Contact section
- Download portfolio button

## Technical Requirements
- Pure HTML + CSS, zero JS dependencies
- Inline lightbox with CSS-only or minimal inline JS
- AVIF images
- Responsive mobile-first
- Accessible
- SEO-optimized with Schema.org
