About

Design in colour

I'm Holly Matthews, a graphic design student who believes colour is a language. Every hue, every tone, every contrast tells part of the story. This portfolio is built on a split-complementary palette — three colours that clash and harmonise in equal measure.

Brand identity, editorial design, motion graphics, packaging, digital illustration — five disciplines, one approach: intentional, considered, colourful.
