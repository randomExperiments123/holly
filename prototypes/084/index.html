I'm Holly Matthews — a graphic design student who believes the best layouts earn their imbalance. Every off-centre element, every overlapping edge, every deliberately broken rule — they all serve the same goal: making you look twice.

Brand identity, editorial, motion, packaging, illustration. Five ways of saying the same thing: design that doesn't play it safe.
