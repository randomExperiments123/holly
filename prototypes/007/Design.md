# Design Document — Prototype 007

## Concept
Japanese minimalism — the beauty of emptiness and imperfection. Inspired by traditional Japanese aesthetics and modern Japanese design.

## Color Palette
- Background: #f5f0eb (warm paper)
- Text: #2c2420 (sumi ink), #8a7f7a (faded ink)
- Accent: #c43a31 (vermilion red), #5b7b5a (sage green)
- Surface: #ffffff (white)

## Typography
- 'Noto Serif JP' + 'Inter' pairing
- Serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Japanese-style numerals
- Refined letter-spacing

## Layout
- Asymmetric, off-center compositions
- Generous negative space (ma)
- Vertical text elements
- Natural materials feel (paper texture)
- Grid with intentional breaks
