# PRD — Prototype 007: Japanese Minimal

## Overview
Japanese-inspired minimalist portfolio. Zen aesthetic, muted earth tones, asymmetric layout, refined typography.

## Design Direction
- Japanese design principles: ma (negative space), wabi-sabi
- Muted, earthy color palette
- Clean serif and sans-serif pairing
- Asymmetric, off-grid layouts
- Natural textures and subtle patterns
- Minimal ornamentation
