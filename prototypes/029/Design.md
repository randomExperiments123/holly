# Design Document — Prototype 029

## Concept
Neon Noir — a visual style borrowing from cyber-noir films. The page feels like walking through a rain-soaked city at night, with reflections and neon signs casting their glow.

## Color Palette
- Background: #0a0a0f (deep void black)
- Primary Neon: #ff1744 (red), #00e5ff (cyan), #9c27b0 (purple)
- Text: #e0e0e0 (light gray), #888 (muted)
- Card backgrounds: rgba(255,255,255,0.03) with neon borders

## Typography
- Rajdhani (Google Fonts) — clean, geometric sans-serif with a technical feel
- All-caps headings for impact
- Generous letter-spacing for headings

## Layout
- Full-screen hero with animated rain effect (CSS only)
- Two-column project grid on desktop, single on mobile
- Cards with neon border accents and glow on hover
- Fixed translucent nav with backdrop blur

## Key Interactions
- Neon glow animations on hover (box-shadow transitions)
- CSS-only rain effect in hero background
- Lightbox with keyboard navigation
- Smooth scroll navigation
