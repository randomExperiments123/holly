# Product Requirements Document — Prototype 029

## Overview
Neon Noir — a dark, cinematic portfolio inspired by rainy city nights and neon signs. Moody atmosphere with glowing text effects.

## Design Direction
- Dark rainy city aesthetic with neon glow
- High contrast: deep black (#0a0a0f) with vibrant cyan, red, purple neons
- Text shadows that simulate neon tube glow
- Cinematic, moody, dramatic presentation

## Content
- 5 design projects: Nordica Coffee, Urban Perspectives, Motion & Interaction, Botanic, Dreamscapes
- About section
- Contact section
- Download portfolio button

## Technical Requirements
- Pure HTML + CSS, zero JS dependencies
- Inline lightbox with minimal inline JS
- AVIF images
- Responsive mobile-first
- Accessible
- SEO-optimized with Schema.org
