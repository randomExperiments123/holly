# PRD — Prototype 009: Bauhaus Geometric

## Overview
Bauhaus-inspired geometric portfolio. Primary colors, bold geometric shapes, asymmetric compositions, and functionalist design philosophy.

## Design Direction
- Bauhaus/constructivist aesthetic
- Primary colors (red, yellow, blue)
- Geometric shapes (circles, squares, lines)
- Asymmetric, dynamic compositions
- Sans-serif typography ( geometric)
- Form follows function
