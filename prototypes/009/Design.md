# Design Document — Prototype 009

## Concept
Bauhaus geometric — celebrating the intersection of art, craft, and technology through primary colors, geometric abstraction, and functional design.

## Color Palette
- Background: #f5f2eb (warm off-white)
- Primary: #e30613 (Bauhaus red)
- Secondary: #0039a6 (Bauhaus blue)
- Accent: #ffd700 (Bauhaus yellow)
- Text: #1a1a1a, #666

## Typography
- 'Space Grotesk' — geometric sans-serif
- Mixed weights for contrast
- All-caps labels
- Large scale headings

## Layout
- Asymmetric grid with geometric dividers
- Circular and rectangular shape overlays
- Diagonal lines as section breaks
- Bold borders and frames
- Collage-like compositions
