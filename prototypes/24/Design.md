# Design Document — Prototype 024

## Concept
The energy of street art and graffiti culture translated into web design. Bold, unapologetic colors scream from a dark canvas. Spray-paint drips, splatter effects, and raw typographic treatments create the feeling of walking through an urban art gallery.

## Color Palette
- Background: #1a1a1a (dark grey-black)
- Primary: #ff2d55 (vibrant pink)
- Secondary: #00e5ff (cyan)
- Accent: #ffd700 (yellow)
- Text: #ffffff on dark, #1a1a1a on light
- Additional: #ff6b35 (orange)

## Typography
- Headings: 'Bangers', cursive (bold, comic/graffiti feel)
- Body: 'Rajdhani', sans-serif (clean, urban)
- Bangers brings authentic graffiti lettering
- Rajdhani is clean and readable with urban edge

## Layout
- Dark background with layered color blocks
- Diagonal/angled section dividers
- Centered max-width container (1100px)
- Two-column grid on desktop, single on mobile
- Fixed header with graffiti-style logo
- Cards with bold borders and color accents
- CSS splatter/drip pseudo-element effects

## Key Interactions
- Card hover: spray-paint splatter reveal
- Color flash on interaction
- Inline lightbox for project images
- Smooth scroll navigation
