Chapter 01

Where it begins

Every design has a starting point — a spark, a question, a material to shape. I'm Holly Matthews, a graphic design student compelled by the space where story meets form. My work moves between brand identity, editorial systems, motion, packaging, and digital illustration, always chasing the intersection of clarity and wonder.

This portfolio is a map of that pursuit: five projects, each a chapter in an ongoing conversation between maker and medium.

Chapter 03

The process behind the work

Every project starts the same way: with questions. What is this for? Who is it for? What does it need to say? From there, it's a process of iteration — sketching, testing, discarding, refining — until the form matches the intent.

I believe great design is invisible when it works. It doesn't shout. It creates a space where the content can be heard. That principle guides every decision, from the weight of a typeface to the margin around an image.
