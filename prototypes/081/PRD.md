# PRD: 081 — Scroll Story

## Overview
A narrative-driven portfolio that unfolds as the user scrolls. Parallax layers, illustrated section backgrounds, and chapter-like divisions tell Holly's story as a journey.

## Requirements
- Long vertical scroll with parallax depth effects on background layers
- Chapter-like sections with large section numbers (01, 02, etc.)
- Narrative prose woven throughout the portfolio
- Illustrated/graphic background motifs per section
- Smooth scroll-triggered fade/reveal animations
- All 5 project cards presented as "case studies" within the narrative
- Standard lightbox for project images
- Mobile-first, responsive, accessible

## Content Structure
1. Hero — "Holly Matthews / Design Journey" with parallax background
2. Chapter 01 — Origin / About with illustrated background
3. Chapter 02 — Projects (the 5 portfolio pieces presented as story beats)
4. Chapter 03 — Process / Approach
5. Chapter 04 — Contact / Connect
6. Footer

## Constraints
- Pure HTML+CSS+inline JS
- AVIF images only
- No external dependencies
- Must pass accessibility checks
