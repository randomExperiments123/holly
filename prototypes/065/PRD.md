# Product Requirements Document — Prototype 065

## Concept
Luxury marble portfolio. Premium marble textures, elegant veining, gold accents, white/gray/rose gold palette. High-end fashion/luxury brand aesthetic with serif typography.

## Design Direction
- Inspiration: High-end marble surfaces, luxury fashion brands, premium stationery
- Marble veining as background texture (CSS gradients)
- Gold (#D4AF37) and Rose Gold (#B76E79) metallic accents
- White (#FAFAFA), Gray (#E0DED7), Charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- Serif typography throughout — elegant, timeless
- Minimalist with material richness

## Content
- 5 design projects with the standard project data
- About section
- Contact section
- Download portfolio button

## Technical Requirements
- Pure HTML + CSS, zero JS dependencies
- Inline lightbox with minimal inline JS
- AVIF images
- Responsive mobile-first
- Accessible
- SEO-optimized with Schema.org
