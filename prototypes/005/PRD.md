# PRD — Prototype 005: Dark Neubrutalism

## Overview
Bold, dark neubrutalist portfolio with heavy typography, thick borders, high contrast, and raw, unfiltered design aesthetic.

## Design Direction
- Dark background (#0d0d0d), white text
- Thick borders (3-4px solid #fff)
- Oversized, heavy typography
- High contrast, no subtlety
- Raw, honest, unpolished aesthetic
- Asymmetric layout elements
