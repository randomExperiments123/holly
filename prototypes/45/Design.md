# Design Decisions — Prototype 045

## Concept
A second interpretation of Bauhaus design that differs significantly from prototype 009. Instead of the grid-based approach, this uses a bold single-column layout with massive geometric elements as section headers.

## Layout
- Single-column throughout (even on desktop)
- Large yellow circle spans the hero section
- Red square and blue triangle serve as section markers
- Asymmetric card layout within the single column

## Typography
Space Grotesk was chosen for its geometric, slightly condensed letterforms that echo Bauhaus typography while feeling contemporary.

## Color Usage
The classic Bauhaus palette of yellow, red, and blue is applied boldly — the yellow circle anchors the page, red appears as accent squares, and blue is used for interactive elements and rules.

## Lightbox
Dark-themed lightbox with geometric frame elements matching the Bauhaus aesthetic.
