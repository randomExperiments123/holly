# Design Document: 082 — Loud Typography

## Concept
Type as protagonist. The page is a typographic performance — words are large, bold, and kinetic. The visual hierarchy is built entirely through scale and weight, with images playing a supporting role.

## Color Palette
- Background: #0a0a0a (near-black)
- Text: #ffffff (white)
- Accent 1: #ff2d55 (vibrant pink-red)
- Accent 2: #00e5ff (cyan)
- Accent 3: #ffeb3b (yellow)

## Typography
- Primary: "Impact", "Arial Black", sans-serif (headlines)
- Body: -apple-system, sans-serif (small labels only)
- Sizes: 14px up to 20vw (hero character fills viewport)
- Weight extremes: 900 for hero, 300 for delicate contrast

## Interactions
- On load: words in hero cascade down from off-screen
- Hover: project titles scale up and shift color
- Staggered reveal: text blocks fade+slight slide on scroll
- CTA buttons: throb/pulse subtly

## Layout
- Hero: single massive character or word, split across lines
- Projects: grid with large number + title, small image
- Asymmetrical text placement — words break out of containers
- Contact: massive "GET IN TOUCH" fills section

## Key Design Decisions
- Pink+cyan+ yellow for pop energy — like a concert poster
- Extreme size contrast for drama
- Words occasionally break out of their grid (overflow visible)
- No decorative imagery — type is the decoration
