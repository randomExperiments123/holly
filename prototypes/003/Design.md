# Design Document — Prototype 003

## Concept
Glassmorphism — the art of frosted glass. Deep, vibrant gradients serve as backgrounds while content sits on translucent glass panels with blurred backdrop support.

## Color Palette
- Background gradient: #667eea → #764ba2 (purple-blue)
- Glass cards: rgba(255,255,255,.15) with backdrop-blur
- Text: #fff (primary), rgba(255,255,255,.7) (secondary)
- Accents: #f093fb, #f5576c

## Typography
- 'Outfit' from Google Fonts (modern, geometric)
- Clean sans-serif with rounded terminals
- Generous letter-spacing for headings

## Layout
- Full-screen sections with gradient backgrounds
- Glass cards with subtle white borders
- Rounded corners throughout (16px-24px)
- Generous padding inside glass panels
- Section titles with gradient text
